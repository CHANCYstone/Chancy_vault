### 2. 单词卡：Post

**英文单词：** Post

**词性：**

- 名词 (Noun)
- 动词 (Verb)

**名词含义：**

1. **柱子或标杆** - A long, strong piece of wood or metal set upright as a marker or support.
    
    - 例如：The post indicated the boundary of the property.
2. **邮政系统** - The system by which letters and packages are collected, sorted, and delivered.
    
    - 例如：She sent the letter by post.
3. **职位** - A job, especially one in the public service or administration.
    
    - 例如：He was appointed to a post in the government.

**动词含义：**

1. **邮寄** - To send something by mail.
    
    - 例如：I will post the letter tomorrow.
2. **公布** - To put a message or notice on a public notice board or online.
    
    - 例如：She posted a notice about the meeting.

**词源：** Middle English: from Old French poster, from Latin postis ‘door, doorpost’.

### 3 单词卡：Poster

**英文单词：** Poster

**词性：** 名词 (Noun)

**含义：**

1. **海报** - A large printed picture or notice used for decoration or advertisement.
    
    - 例如：The band is promoting their new album with a series of posters.
2. **张贴者** - Someone who posts messages or notices, especially online.
    
    - 例如：He’s a frequent poster on the forum.

**派生：** From ‘post’ + ‘-er’ (suffix indicating someone or something that does an action).

**词源：** Late Middle English: from medieval Latin postarius, from Latin posta (plural) ‘station, post’, reinforced by Old French poster ‘poster, posterer’ and posteur ‘poster’.

### 4. 单词卡：Primarily
基本信息：
- 英文单词：Primarily
- 中文翻译：主要地；首先
- 词性：副词
- 音标：[praɪˈmɛrəli]
例句：
- Primarily, the study focuses on the impact of climate change.
（首先，这项研究主要关注气候变化的影响。）
同义词：
- Mainly
- Chiefly
- Mostly
反义词：
- Secondarily
- Incidentally
