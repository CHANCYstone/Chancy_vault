## Scientific contributions


# In predictive econometric modeling 

Diebold is best known for the "Diebold–Mariano test" for comparing point forecast accuracy,[[4]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-4) methods for assessing density forecast conditional calibration,[[5]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-5) and for his text, _Elements of Forecasting_.[[6]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-6)

# In financial econometrics 
Diebold is best known for his contributions in financial asset return volatility modeling, especially the Andersen-Bollerslev-Diebold analyses of "realized volatility" extracted from high-frequency asset returns,[[7]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-7)[[8]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-8) for the Diebold–Li "dynamic Nelson-Siegel" yield-curve model and its extensions,[[9]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-9)[[10]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-10)[[11]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-DieboldRudebusch2013-11) and for the Diebold-Nerlove latent-factor ARCH model.[[12]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-12)

# In macroeconometrics
Diebold is best known for his work on the macro-finance interface,[[13]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-13)[[14]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-14) for his work empirically integrating linear dynamic factor modeling with nonlinear regime switching,[[15]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-15) and for his work on [real-time macroeconomic monitoring](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nowcasting_\(economics\) "Nowcasting (economics)"), particularly the [Aruoba–Diebold–Scotti ("ADS") Business Conditions Index](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aruoba%E2%80%93Diebold%E2%80%93Scotti_Index "Aruoba–Diebold–Scotti Index"), now maintained by the [Federal Reserve Bank of Philadelphia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_Reserve_Bank_of_Philadelphia "Federal Reserve Bank of Philadelphia").[[16]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-16)[[17]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-17)

# In network analysis, 

Diebold is best known for the Diebold–Yilmaz framework for dynamic network connectedness measurement and visualization.[[18]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-18) [[19]](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Francis_X._Diebold#cite_note-19)
