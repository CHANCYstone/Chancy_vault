# 结论 (Conclusion)
First, elastic net applies shrinkage mainly on small coefficients
forcing them to zero, therefore, underestimates smaller ties be-
tween sovereigns.

Second, the higher coefficients are expected to be scaled down
according to their sizes, therefore, relative importance of domes-
tic and global factors does not necessarily change.

Third, the reader may inquire whether adaptive elastic net can
be used on VAR estimation as it is used on simple linear regres-
sions.
